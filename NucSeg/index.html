A Multi-site Dataset for Histology Nuclei Segmentation

This is a well-organized multi-site dataset for histology nuclei segmentation, which contains H&E stained tissue images with segmentation masks collected from three public datasets.

It can support research in various problem settings with need of multi-site data, such as Federated Learning, Domain Generalization, Multi-site Learning and Continual Learning, etc.

Among these data:
  • Samples of Site A,B,C,D are from MoNuSAC2020 challenge dataset [1].
  • Samples of Site E are from MoNuSAC2018 challenge dataset [2].
  • Samples of Site F are from Deep Regression For Nuclei Segmentation (DRFNS) [3].

(3) Preprocessing steps

Based on the original data trian-test split, we further split out 20% of training data for validation.
We have converted the annotation files fom '.xml' to '.png' format. For preprocessing, we resize all samples in the six sites to size of 256x256.
